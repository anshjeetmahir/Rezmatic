how -to- Guides

  1. Which Hair Treatment is Good for Hair?

    It is difficult to recommend a specific hair treatment without knowing more about the individual's hair type, condition, and desired results. There are many different types of hair treatments available that are formulated to address specific hair concerns such as dryness, damage, or frizz. It is best to consult with a professional stylist or dermatologist to determine the most suitable hair treatment for your individual needs. They will be able to recommend a treatment based on your hair type, condition, and desired results.

  2. What Types of Hair Treatment Services does Vurve Signature Salon Offer?

    Vurve Signature Salon offers a range of hair treatment services such as smoothing, perming, and anti-frizz treatments.

  3. Are There Any Special Considerations for Treating Treated or Damaged Hair?

    Treating treated or damaged hair may require extra care to avoid further damage. It is important to discuss the condition of your hair with the stylist before the service begins to determine the best course of action. Vurve Signature Salon offers specialised treatments or products to help nourish and protect treated or damaged hair during the treatment process.

  4. What is a Kerastase Ritual?

    A Kerastase ritual is a specialised hair treatment service offered by Vurve Signature Salon that includes a range of products and techniques to nourish and revitalise the hair.

  5. Are There Different Kerastase Rituals Available at Vurve Signature Salon?

    Vurve Signature Salon offers a variety of Kerastase rituals to choose from, each designed to address specific hair concerns such as dryness, damage, or frizz. Some of them include K Water Blow Dry, Fusio Scrub, Instant Conditioning Fusio Dose, Scalp Care, Experience Ritual, Curl Defining Ritual, etc

  6. Can I Request a Specific Hair Treatment Brand at Vurve Signature Salon?

    Vurve Signature Salon offers a selection of different hair treatment brands to choose from. It is always a good idea to discuss your preferences and any specific concerns with the stylist before the service begins. The stylist will be able to recommend the best hair treatment brand for your individual needs and desired result.

  7. How Often Should I Get a Hair Treatment at Vurve Signature Salon?

    The frequency of hair treatment services will depend on the specific treatment and the condition of the hair. It is best to discuss a suitable schedule for treatment with the stylist based on your individual needs and desired results
