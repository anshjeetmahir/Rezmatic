FAQs
  • Which makeup is good for engagement?

    We recommend HD Makeup if you want a less dramatic and natural-looking, glowy skin base. For a longer lasting flawless look that feels light yet gives an incandescent appearance on the camera, we recommend Airbrush makeup.

    It takes a minimum of 3 hours to complete the engagement makeup.
